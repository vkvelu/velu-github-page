Nationwide Children's Hospital | Institute for Genomic Medicine

VarLink Genomics Integration

Architect and implement Java-based components that receive genomic JSON payloads, validate variant data, transform domain-specific attributes into HL7 ORU messages, and support delivery to Epic Beaker through LabVantage LIMS and Cloverleaf workflows.

Key outcomes

  • Rapidly developed the genomics-domain and platform expertise needed to deliver across LabVantage LIMS, Cloverleaf, and Epic Beaker.
  • Designed an annotation-driven mapping between gene-variant and Beaker attributes, reducing the code required to introduce new mappings.
  • Built an exception-resilient journaling system that records receipt, parsing, validation, HL7 encoding, and dispatch events as JSON in both log files and the LIMS database for end-to-end traceability and diagnosis.
  • Delivered VarLink for production deployment after unit, integration, end-to-end, and user-acceptance testing completed with zero reported errors.

Founded and led an AI-enabled chemical informatics platform for molecular drawing, structure search, analytics, 3D visualization, multilingual scientific documentation, collaboration, and reusable research knowledge.

  • Led a globally distributed team spanning the United States, Spain, Ecuador, Colombia, Egypt, France, and India, maintaining alignment and delivery through a consistent daily working cadence.
